SHEERSTOCK PARK · Oil Desk News Terminal
A live, AI-powered news & market-intelligence platform for the oil desk — dashboard + automated email briefings.

It watches 50+ news sources around the clock, scores every headline for oil-relevance and market impact with AI, and surfaces what actually matters — on a live dashboard and in twice-daily email briefings, with instant alerts when something big breaks.

1 · The Dashboard

A live website you can open any time — it updates itself continuously, even when no one's watching.

Access is private — you log in once with the account we set up. It works on desktop and phone.

What's on it

🏠 Overview — the landing page: the biggest issues across all areas, a live AI "desk read" of the day, plus a click-to-browse view by area (Macro, Oil, Fuel, Geopolitics, Equities, Shipping).

📋 Brief — generate the AI market briefing on demand, and ask plain-English questions about the news ("what's the latest on Hormuz?").

📰 Feed — the full searchable archive of every story, filterable by source, topic and relevance.

📊 Pulse — trends and sentiment analytics across the coverage.

🗣️ Voices — what key figures (Trump, OPEC, officials) are saying.

🛢️ Markets — live price tape (Brent, WTI, products, FX), key spreads, price history with news overlaid, and EIA inventory data.

🌍 Flows & Reserves — US strategic & commercial oil reserves, US production/imports/exports, and global crude production by country.

⚑ Always on top — key upcoming market events (EIA, OPEC, CPI, Fed, jobs) and live Gulf/tropical-storm risk to oil production.

2 · The Email Briefings

Daily briefings — 06:00 & 20:00 UK

A sharp, trader-voice market note written fresh by AI from that moment's news. Leads with the biggest headlines, then breaks down Energy & Fuel, Geopolitics, Macro & Rates, Market Movers, Reserves, and what to watch next — with the live price tape, clickable links to the source articles, and the upcoming market events.

Urgent alerts — only when it matters

Intraday emails reserved for genuinely big breaks — a tanker strike in Hormuz, a surprise OPEC move, an SPR release, a Gulf hurricane, a rate shock. The subject line is the headline itself, so you know at a glance. Quiet days send nothing; it's a watchdog, not a newsletter.

4 · Good to know

  • Data: 50+ news sources; live prices (≈15-min delayed free feed, clearly labelled); official US energy data (EIA); weather (NOAA); economic calendar (US gov data).
  • Honest by design: AI impact reads are decision-support, not investment advice — and everything is labelled with how fresh it is.
  • Optional future add-ons: genuinely real-time prices (paid feed, ~$15–30/mo) and a live tanker-tracking map (~$20–100/mo) — nice-to-haves, not needed for the core.

5 · Under the hood — how it works

The whole system runs itself in the cloud — nothing to install, no servers to manage.

  • Collects — pulls headlines from 50+ sources into a cloud database around the clock, every few minutes.
  • Scores — AI (Claude) reads each story and rates it for oil-relevance and market impact, refreshed hourly.
  • Shows — the dashboard reads that live database, so it's always current — even at 3am with no one watching.
  • Sends — automated emails: scheduled briefings at 06:00 & 20:00, plus urgent alerts the moment something big breaks.
